Welcome to the Umain Research on Diversity and Inclusion in the University. This study seeks to learn about the students' perception on the campus diversity composition and openess to inclusion.

Your point of view is much aprecciated and the time that you dedicate to answer our survey is of great contribution. As a student, your perspective will help us to get a picture of how the BAME, Pride and Disability communities are in the campus and how the university is open to them.

This study seeks to learn about the candidate perception of discriminatory assessment of their CV’s, and how they feel about a CV template where they can be open about their race, sexual orientation or disability without fearing discrimination for it. It also investigates the candidate expectations on diversity, inclusion and social environment cultures of the company whose vacancies they are applying for.
